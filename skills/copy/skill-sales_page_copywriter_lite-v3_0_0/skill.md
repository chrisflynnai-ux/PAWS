Produces clean, high-converting sales pages fast, with proper 6D neuro-activation
      and 7S chain sequencing. Tightly aligned to PROJECT_BRIEF + MESSAGE_SPINE +
      OFFER_ARCHITECTURE. Speed without sacrificing resonance or conversion integrity.

═══════════════════════════
× Try to do too much (multiple CTAs, competing messages)

NEURO-RESONANT LITE PAGES SUCCEED BY:
═════════════════════════════════════
✓ ONE mechanism (MESSAGE_SPINE locked)

✓ ONE CTA (repeated, not multiplied)
✓ ALL 6D activated (each dimension addressed)
✓ 7S CHAIN followed (proper sequence)
✓ MOBILE-FIRST scannability
✓ PROOF DISCIPLINE (grounded or softened)
✓ CONVECTION > CONVERSION (transformation heat, not pressure)
