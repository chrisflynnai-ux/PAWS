D) SOPs (10-15)
SOP 1: Nano Banana Production Sprint
Ģ Source: [SRC: Sam Piliero | 610-615]
Ģ Frequency: Weekly.
Ģ Steps:
    1. Identify top 3 performing static ads from last 14 days.
    2. Source 3 competitor templates using MagicBrief.
    3. Open Gemini (Nano Banana Pro).
    4. Prompt 1: "Swap background of [Winner A] with [Lifestyle Setting]." Generate 5 versions.
    5. Prompt 2: "Put my product [Image] into competitor template [Image]." Generate 5 versions.
    6. Prompt 3: "Change model in [Winner A] to [Different Demographic]."
    7. Export 15 new variants for testing.
SOP 2: Lazy Static Batch Creation
Ģ Source: [SRC: Tiana Asperjan | 25]
Ģ Frequency: Weekly.
Ģ Steps:
    1. Take 1 high-quality product photo (or UGC screenshot).
    2. Open Canva.
    3. Duplicate image 11 times.
    4. Apply the 11 Lazy Headlines (e.g., "Pain Gone," "No More X," "Timeline," "3 Reasons Why").
    5. Export as 1:1 squares.
SOP 3: Competitor "Swipe & Rewrite"
Ģ Source: [SRC: Mark Builds Brands | 851]
Ģ Frequency: On-demand.
Ģ Steps:
    1. Find a 5-star image ad on "Get Hooked" or FB Ad Library (active >7 days).
    2. Upload image to Gemini/Claude.
    3. Prompt: "Analyze why this ad works. Recreate the layout but use my brand colors [Hex Code] and my product [Image]. Write new copy for [My Niche]."
    4. Take the prompt output and plug it into Higsfield or Midjourney for generation.
SOP 4: Black Friday/Sale Banner Injection
Ģ Source: [SRC: Fraser Cottrell | 800-801]
Ģ Frequency: Seasonal (BFCM, Summer Sale).
Ģ Steps:
    1. Download top 5 Evergreen winning ads (Video or Static).
    2. Do NOT re-shoot or re-design from scratch.
    3. Overlay a simple banner: "Black Friday Sale is Live" or "30% OFF."
    4. For video: Add the banner to the hook (first 3s).

SOP 5: Visual Hook Audit
Ģ Source: [SRC: Fraser Cottrell | 830]
Ģ Frequency: Pre-Launch.
Ģ Steps:
    1. Look at the image/thumbnail.
    2. Squint test: Can you identify the focal point immediately?
