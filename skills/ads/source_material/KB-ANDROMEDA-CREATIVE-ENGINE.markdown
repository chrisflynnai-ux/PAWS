L2_CORE_MECHANISMS:
  - name: "Andromeda Retrieval Engine"
    explanation: "Meta's AI architecture that matches ad content to user psychographics based on engagement signals rather than demographic inputs."
    implication: "Broad targeting is required; specific hooks (e.g., 'If you have back pain') act as the targeting filter."
    quote: "The creative itself does the targeting so whoever you call out in the ad the ad is going to get served in front of [SRC: Dr. Matt Shiver | 53]."

  - name: "Concept Over Variation"
    explanation: "The algorithm penalizes repetitive formats. Testing must involve distinct concepts (e.g., Unboxing vs. Skit), not minor variations (e.g., red vs. blue)."
    implication: "Do not waste budget on button color tests; test entirely different angles."
    quote: "Facebook doesn't like you creating a ton of different variations they want to see concepts instead [SRC: Alex Robinson | 120]."

  - name: "Post ID Harvesting"
    explanation: "Taking the specific Post ID of a winning ad from a testing campaign and moving it to a scaling campaign to preserve likes, comments, and social proof."
    implication: "Lowers CPM and increases trust/conversion rate."
    quote: "You've isolated the unicorn... and you've added it to your all-star team [SRC: Professor Charley T | 105]."

  - name: "3:2:2 Flexible Ad System"

    steps:
      1. "Context Lean: Establish topic (e.g., 'The Sphere is insane')."
      2. "Interjection: 'But get this...' (Stun gun)."

L2_DECISION_HEURISTICS:
  DIAGNOSTIC:
    - "IF Hook Rate is <25%, THEN audit the Visual Hook (first 3s) for 'Visual Chaos' or lack of focal point [SRC: Fraser Cottrell | 811]."
    - "IF Hook Rate is >35% but Hold Rate is <15%, THEN you have a 'Clickbait' issue; the video body does not deliver on the hook's promise [SRC: Fraser Cottrell | 424]."
    - "IF CPMs spike >50% week-over-week, THEN your creative diversity is low; launch a new 'Concept' immediately [SRC: THE ECOM KING | 372]."
    - "IF CTR is high (>2%) but Conversion Rate is low, THEN the ad-to-lander congruency is broken; check the 'Post-Click' experience [SRC: blake ecom | 415]."
  OPTIMIZATION:
    - "IF a static ad performs well, THEN use AI (Nano Banana) to generate 10 variations of model/background immediately [SRC: Sam Piliero | 617]."
    - "IF you are under $1M revenue, THEN focus on ONE avatar with multiple angles, rather than multiple avatars [SRC: Dr. Matt Shiver | 54]."
    - "IF scaling a winner, THEN do not turn off the original; create a new 'Pack' to run alongside it [SRC: Sam Piliero | 509]."
  SCALING:
    - "IF spending $10k/month, THEN produce at least 1 new ad per week (Rule of 1:10k) [SRC: Sam Piliero | 190]."
    - "IF an ad has high 'Incremental Attribution', THEN it is a true scaler; graduate it to the scaling campaign [SRC: Sam Piliero | 191]."
  TROUBLESHOOTING:
    - "IF viewers drop off after the hook, THEN insert a 'Scroll Stop Interjection' (e.g., 'But get this...') to re-engage [SRC: Kallaway | 352]."
    - "IF static ads are failing, THEN test 'Native' formats (Apple Notes, Twitter screenshots, News Articles) [SRC: Omar Eddaoudi | 268]."

L3_FAILURE_MODES:
  - name: "Creative Fatigue (Andromeda)"
    symptoms: "Rising CPA, dropping CTR on previously winning ads."
    root_cause: "Audience saturation of a specific format; algorithm penalizes repetition."
    recovery: "Launch a new 'Concept' (not variation) in a fresh pack."
    prevention: "Rule of 1:10k production cadence."
  - name: "Hook-Body Mismatch"
    symptoms: "High Hook Rate (>40%), low Hold Rate, low ROAS."
    root_cause: "Clickbait hook sets expectations the product/video cannot fulfill."
    recovery: "Re-edit the video body to match the hook's intensity or tone."
  - name: "The 'Polished' Trap"
    symptoms: "High production value ads getting low CTR."
    root_cause: "Ads look like ads; users scroll past commercial content."
    recovery: "Switch to 'Ugly' ads, native formats, or founder-shot content [SRC: Justin Lalonde | 22]."
  - name: "Variation Testing Loop"
    symptoms: "Testing 50 ads but performance remains flat."
    root_cause: "Testing button colors or small text tweaks instead of distinct Concepts."
    recovery: "Adopt the 'Concept vs. Variation' protocol [SRC: Alex Robinson | 120]."
