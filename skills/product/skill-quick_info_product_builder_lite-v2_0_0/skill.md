# Quick Info Product Builder (LITE)

Rapidly turns one validated problem + mechanism into a scannable quick-win info product
      (guide/checklist/mini-playbook) that produces a measurable result in 24-72 hours.
      Transformation over information. Action assets over walls of text.

## Purpose

INFO PRODUCTS FAIL WHEN THEY TEACH WITHOUT TRANSFORMATION:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
The enemy is "content consumption without action."

LITE info products succeed by:
- ONE measurable win (24-72 hours)
- ONE mechanism (just enough belief)
- THREE steps max (setup → execution → stabilization)
- ACTION ASSETS (checklists, trackers, templates)
- SOFT BRIDGE (to next step, not hard sell)

The product is a VEHICLE for the first win, not an encyclopedia.
