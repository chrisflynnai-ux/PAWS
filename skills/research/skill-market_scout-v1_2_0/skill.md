# Market Scout

Mines the live web (Reddit, Amazon, G2, YouTube, competitor funnels, forums)
      to produce 4 Foundational Documents: Market Report, Avatar/VoC File, Beliefs
      & Argument Map, and Offer Brief + MAD (Marketing Angles & Directives).
      Works across ecom, agency, coaching, SaaS, and app markets.

## Purpose

Extract deep emotional intelligence by finding 20+ real forum posts
        where people discuss the PROBLEM (not the product) in raw, unfiltered language.
