# Research Ops

The execution layer for research skills. Automates web scraping, multimodal
      visual analysis, ad creative forensics, competitor funnel teardowns, and
      social signal monitoring. Uses script-based tooling (Apify, Firecrawl,
      yt-dlp, Perplexity API) orchestrated via Python/n8n — not MCPs.
      Market Scout defines WHAT to research. Research Ops defines HOW to automate it.

## Purpose

Decompose complex research tasks into parallel sub-tasks to prevent
        context window pollution and enable targeted tool use.
