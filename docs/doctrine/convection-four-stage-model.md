# Adjunct Note - Convection and the Four-Stage Model

This note updates older constitutional and doctrine materials without requiring a full archive rewrite first.

It should be treated as an active interpretive layer over older documents such as the Resonance Constitution and Neuro Integration materials.

## Directive 1: Convection Over Conversion

The goal is no longer narrow conversion thinking.

The goal is **convection**:

- heat
- enthusiasm
- excitement
- momentum
- advocacy
- sharing
- referrals
- upsell readiness
- durable relationship energy

Conversion is still a checkpoint.
Convection is the actual objective.

The system should optimize for buyers, users, and participants who are:

- excited by the result
- engaged with the experience
- more likely to continue
- more likely to share
- more likely to upgrade
- more likely to become advocates

This shifts the lens from:

- "Did they convert?"

to:

- "Did the interaction create enough heat and value that the relationship advances?"

## The Updated Persuasion Ladder

The current constitutional sequence is:

1. **Resonate -> Persuade**
2. **Impact -> Influence**
3. **Compel -> Convert**
4. **Enhance -> Advance**

This is the active model going forward.

### Stage 1: Resonate -> Persuade

Goal:

- establish relevance
- create trust
- reflect the person's reality
- make them feel understood

What it does:

- opens attention
- lowers resistance
- creates emotional permission to continue

### Stage 2: Impact -> Influence

Goal:

- create meaningful shift
- deliver insight
- make the mechanism or opportunity feel real

What it does:

- moves from interest to changed perspective
- turns attention into directional belief

### Stage 3: Compel -> Convert

Goal:

- make the next step feel aligned, clear, and worthwhile
- mobilize decision and action without violating trust

What it does:

- converts formed conviction into committed movement

### Stage 4: Enhance -> Advance

Goal:

- improve the relationship after the initial commitment
- deepen results
- create momentum for continuation, advocacy, and expansion

What it does:

- turns a buyer into an advancing participant
- turns a participant into a promoter or long-term collaborator

## Directive 2: Simplify the Neuro-Box Into Four Sequential Stair Steps

The deeper neuro-box / hologon architecture remains a valuable archive and advanced design reference.

However, for runtime execution, skill design, evaluation, and communication, it should now be simplified into a **four-stage sequential stair-step model**.

Each step has **two parts**, matching the updated persuasion ladder.

## The Four Stair Steps

### Step 1: Resonate + Persuade

Foundation:

- safety
- relevance
- empathy
- trust

Runtime interpretation:

- identify the real pain, desire, or situation
- frame it accurately enough that the person feels seen

This is the entry step.
Nothing downstream works if this fails.

### Step 2: Impact + Influence

Formation:

- insight
- understanding
- mechanism
- importance

Runtime interpretation:

- create a shift in how the person sees the problem, opportunity, or path
- strengthen belief through meaning, clarity, and pattern recognition

This is where analysis becomes synthesis.

### Step 3: Compel + Convert

Commitment:

- decision
- movement
- action
- aligned urgency

Runtime interpretation:

- remove friction
- present the next step clearly
- create enough energy and conviction for forward action

This is where synthesis becomes committed action.

### Step 4: Enhance + Advance

Expansion:

- success
- reinforcement
- advocacy
- continuation

Runtime interpretation:

- improve the experience after the decision
- create stronger outcomes, stronger enthusiasm, and stronger future movement

This is where action becomes convection.

## Practical Interpretation

The full archive architecture can remain rich and multidimensional behind the scenes.

But the build-facing doctrine should now use this simplified operational ladder:

```text
1. Resonate -> Persuade
2. Impact -> Influence
3. Compel -> Convert
4. Enhance -> Advance
```

This is easier to:

- teach
- score
- implement
- route through agents
- package into skill families
- explain to clients and collaborators

## Immediate Build Implications

### ZPWO

ZPWO should eventually support routing artifacts through the four stair steps as a higher-level interpretation layer above detailed track mechanics.

### MMA

MMA should begin to score not only for artifact quality, but for stage fit:

- does it resonate?
- does it influence?
- does it compel?
- does it enhance and advance?

### Skill Builder

All future skill families should be mappable to the four-stage model, even if they still carry deeper domain-specific logic underneath.

### Knowledge Synthesis

New atoms, experiences, and patterns should be classifiable according to which stair step they most strengthen.

## Convection Metrics

Over time, the system should move from conversion metrics alone to convection metrics such as:

- enthusiasm signal
- response energy
- completion depth
- repeat usage
- upgrade readiness
- referrals
- testimonials
- advocacy behavior

These are stronger indicators of durable value than conversion alone.

## Working Rule

Older archive materials are not discarded.
They are interpreted through this simpler operational lens until they are revised.

The simplified doctrine for active build work is:

- Convection over conversion
- Four sequential stair steps over excessive theoretical complexity
- Ethical relational momentum over manipulative pressure
